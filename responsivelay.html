About our hotle

Paro Hotel is a charming accommodation option located in the picturesque town of Paro in Bhutan. The hotel offers a blend of traditional Bhutanese architecture and modern amenities, providing a cozy and comfortable stay for visitors. Guests can enjoy well-appointed rooms with stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. The hotel's design often incorporates local materials and craftsmanship, creating an authentic cultural experience.
